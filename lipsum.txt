A negative test score indicates discord in the relationship
. The couple might be physically attracted to each other, but it is going to be rough. There might be a lot of disagreements 
and may even harm each other. The association will need many adjustments for it to work. 
Read the interpretation in the next section to find out what aspects cause harmony and discord.
A score of +20 and below indicates average harmony. This is a casual relationship from everyday people. 
The pair may say hi and hello to each other, but there is no deep connection between them.
This is sometimes called the “friends zone.”
